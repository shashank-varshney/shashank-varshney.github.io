Stream cipher
a new checkpointable stream cipher mechanism that is a hybrid of block and stream ciphers. It combines the advantages of both by creating a mechanism to regularly checkpoint the internal state of a stream cipher. The state is encrypted using block ciphers and this gives us the benefit of both the mechanisms. We show that we have a comparable throughput as a fast stream or block ciphers. (Work in progress)
